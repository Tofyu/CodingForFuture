About us

We inspire children's interests to program

COF focuses on developing coding skills and helping students gain a larger appreciation for computer science. We aim to diversify the ethnic populations in coding fields by reaching out to minority groups and providing them with resources, workshops, and opportunities to become a great coder. COF works to provide the inspirational spark to curious children by initiating fun and interactive activities involving basic programming.
